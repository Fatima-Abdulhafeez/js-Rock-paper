Rock Paper Scissors

1. Choose Your Move:
Tap one of the buttons to select your move: 🪨 Rock, 📄 Paper, or ✂️ Scissors.

2. Wait for the Result:
The computer will randomly pick a move.

3. Check Who Wins:
- 🪨 Rock beats ✂️ Scissors
- ✂️ Scissors beats 📄 Paper
- 📄 Paper beats 🪨 Rock
- The same move is a tie!
